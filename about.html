Hi, thanks for visiting my site.

I am Abdul Karim Tawakim, a freelance graphic artist / designer, living in Iligan City, Philippines. I am a freshman in Mindanao State University - Iligan Institute of Technology, taking the course Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT).

In 2018, I really took a focus to Graphic Design and so commenced my career as a freelance graphic designer, I was able to find clients online, locally and internationally, and got commissioned works. I took BSIT as I plan to learn programming, combining it with my skill in graphic design, I can eventually become a Web Designer. And creating and designing this website, is a milestone for me. This website was made in partial fulfillment of the requirements for the academic subject: Fundamentals of Computing, for the course Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, in Mindanao State University-Iligan Institute of Technology.
